Hey, I'm Alice.

I work with technological objects, film, and (sometimes) clay.

At Capital One, my team is currently building a data-as-a-service platform. Previously, I worked on the ComiXology mobile app.

I studied the intersection of design, technnology, and philosophy at NYU Gallatin. My concentration applied affect theory as a lens to examine the relationship between humans and technology and its "irreducible alterity... in and through its active connection to the human and vice versa." (Massumi, Parables for the Virtual)

Yes, I grew up with a Tamagotchi pet (that I lovingly nurtured into a Mametchi).

My creative work continues to explore the transmission of affect in built environments.

A short essay I wrote on mythology, AI , and desire was published in print for CLOG's Artifical Intelligence issue in 2019.

I collect print ephemera and try to maintain a ghostlike presence online.
